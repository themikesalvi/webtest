FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about working with XLNZ Productions

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for smaller projects and 1-2 months for larger productions. However, we understand that sometimes projects come up on short notice, and we'll do our best to accommodate your timeline based on our current schedule.

Do you work with clients outside of Philadelphia?

Yes! While our studio is based in Philadelphia, we work with clients throughout the tri-state area and beyond. For projects outside our immediate area, we may charge additional travel fees, which will be clearly outlined in your project quote.

What's included in your standard packages?

Our packages include professional recording, audio editing & mixing, digital delivery, and strategy consultation. Higher-tier packages include additional services like custom intros/outros, sound design, and color correction. You can view detailed package information on our Packages page.

How long does it take to receive the final product?

Turnaround time varies depending on the scope of your project. Podcast episodes typically take 3-5 business days, while stand-up specials may take 2-3 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the production process.

What is your payment structure?

We typically require a 50% deposit to secure your booking and begin pre-production, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. We accept various payment methods including credit cards, bank transfers, and checks.

Can I be involved in the editing process?

Absolutely! We encourage client collaboration throughout the process. Your package includes a set number of revision rounds, and we provide preview links so you can review and provide feedback. We want to ensure the final product matches your vision perfectly.
