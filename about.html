How It Started

Born from Passion

Founded in 2023 by Buhzz Taylor and Mike Salvi, XLNZ Productions was born from a shared passion for storytelling and a commitment to excellence in video production. What started as a small operation has quickly grown into one of Philadelphia's most comprehensive production facilities.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, professional video production services that are accessible to everyone—from independent creators to established businesses. We believe that great stories deserve to be told beautifully, and we're dedicated to making that happen for every client who walks through our doors.

Our Approach

Craft Meets Technology

At XLNZ Productions, we pride ourselves on our state-of-the-art equipment and facilities. Our studio features multiple recording spaces, editing suites, and a sound stage designed to accommodate projects of any scale. From podcast recording booths to a full film production set, we have everything needed to bring your creative vision to life.

But it's not just about the equipment—it's about the people behind it. Our team of creative professionals brings years of experience in every aspect of production, from concept development to final delivery. We're not just technicians; we're storytellers who are passionate about creating content that resonates with audiences and achieves our clients' goals.

Milestones

Our Journey

Key moments in the XLNZ Productions story

Early 2023

The Idea Takes Shape

Buhzz Taylor and Mike Salvi combine their creative visions and industry experience to conceptualize XLNZ Productions—a studio built on the belief that professional video production should be accessible to all.

Mid 2023

Studio Launch

XLNZ Productions officially opens its doors in Philadelphia, equipped with state-of-the-art recording facilities, editing suites, and a dedicated sound stage ready for any production scale.

Late 2023

First Major Projects

The studio completes its first batch of projects including podcasts, comedy specials, and local business commercials—establishing a reputation for quality and reliability in the Philadelphia market.

Early 2024

Expanding Services

With a growing client base and portfolio, XLNZ expands its service offerings to include social media content packages and testimonial video production, meeting the evolving needs of modern businesses.

Present

Growing Strong

Today, XLNZ Productions continues to push creative boundaries, serving a diverse range of clients while staying true to our founding mission: making professional video production accessible, collaborative, and exceptional.
