How It Started

Born from Purpose

XLNZ Productions was founded in 2023 on a simple but powerful belief that most brands are invisible not because they have a bad story, but because nobody is telling it the right way. Buhzz Taylor and Mike Salvi built XLNZ to change that.

From day one, we approached this differently. We didn't just want to be another production company that hands you a finished file and disappears. We wanted to be the team that actually understands your audience, studies your competition, and builds content with a strategy behind it content that performs, not just content that looks good.

That's still exactly what we do today.

Our Approach

We Don't Just Show Up to Shoot.

Most production companies arrive on shoot day and execute what they're told. We show up having already done the work understanding your brand, your goals, and what kind of content actually moves the needle for businesses like yours.

We think like marketers first. That means every decision we make on set the framing, the pacing, the story arc, the call to action is made with your audience in mind. Then we bring the cinematic quality that makes your brand impossible to scroll past.

From concept development and scripting through production and final delivery, we handle the full process in-house. One team. One vision. No handoffs, no outsourcing, no excuses.

Our Values

The principles that guide every project, every client, every time

Strategy First

We never pick up a camera without a plan. Every project starts with understanding your audience, your goals, and what success actually looks like for your business.

Cinematic Standard

We approach every brand video the way a director approaches a feature film with intention, precision, and a relentless focus on quality that makes your content stand out.

True Partnership

We don't take orders we collaborate. Your vision shapes the direction, and our expertise elevates it. You're involved at every stage because this is your brand.

Reliability

Results-Driven

Always Evolving

The content landscape changes fast. We stay ahead of platform trends, algorithm shifts, and audience behavior so your brand is always ahead of the curve, never chasing it.

Milestones

Our Journey

Key moments in the XLNZ Productions story

Early 2023

The Vision Takes Shape

Buhzz Taylor and Mike Salvi identify a gap in Philadelphia's production market brands with great stories but no team who knows how to tell them strategically on video. XLNZ is born to fill that gap.

November 2023

XLNZ Productions Launches

The company officially opens with a full production setup professional cameras, lighting, audio, and editing capabilities ready to serve clients at any scale from day one.

Late 2023 – 2024

Building the Podcast Roster

XLNZ rapidly builds Philadelphia's most active independent podcast production roster signing 10+ shows and establishing a reputation for quality, reliability, and results that keep clients coming back season after season.

2024 – 2025

Expanding Into Film & Brand Video

With a proven production track record, XLNZ expands into full film production, commercials, and corporate brand video bringing the same cinematic standard to business clients across Philadelphia and beyond.

May 2026

Meet The Sickos Cinemark Premiere

XLNZ independently writes, funds, and produces Philadelphia's first homegrown horror slasher film Meet The Sickos screening at Cinemark in West Philadelphia. A milestone that proves what's possible when the XLNZ standard meets full creative control.

Now

Going Corporate. Going Bigger.

XLNZ is actively expanding into the corporate sector bringing its strategy-first, filmmaker-quality approach to brands and businesses that are ready to stop being invisible and start building a real presence on video.
