FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our packages

How long does production typically take?

Production timelines vary depending on the scope of your project. A single podcast episode typically takes 3-5 days from recording to final delivery, while a stand-up special may take 2-3 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Can I customize a package to fit my specific needs?

Absolutely! Our packages are designed to be starting points. We can customize any package by adding or removing services to better fit your budget and requirements. Contact us to discuss a custom solution for your project.

What payment options do you offer?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. For podcast packages, we offer a flexible payment plan: 25% down to book your dates, first 2 episodes released when your balance reaches 50%, and the full season delivered upon 100% payment.

Do you provide raw footage/audio files?

Yes, all packages include delivery of the final edited files. Raw footage or audio files can be provided for an additional fee. Please let us know in advance if you require raw files, as this affects our workflow and storage requirements.

What's included in the strategy consultation?

Every package includes a strategy consultation where we discuss your goals, target audience, content strategy, and distribution plan. We'll help you understand how to maximize the impact of your content and reach the right viewers.

Can I upgrade my package after starting?

Yes, you can upgrade at any time. If you decide you need additional episodes or services after starting, we'll adjust your package and apply any payments already made toward the upgraded total.
