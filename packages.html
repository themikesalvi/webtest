FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our packages and process

What is the difference between the Corporate and Social Media tabs?

Corporate packages are built for businesses and brands think paid ads, ecommerce, website video, and ongoing brand content strategy. Social Media packages are built for content creators who want to book studio time and shoot short-form skits and sketches. If you are a business, use the Corporate tab. If you are a creator, use the Social Media tab.

How does the corporate retainer work?

We start with a discovery call to understand your brand, goals, and audience. From there we build a content strategy, agree on deliverables, and begin producing on a monthly cycle. Retainers are billed monthly and include a dedicated account contact who manages your relationship with XLNZ from start to finish.

What do I need to bring to a social media session?

Your skit or concept must be fully written before you arrive. Session time is production time and we do not stop the clock for writing. If you need help developing concepts ahead of your session, add our Heavy in the Lab concept creation package and we will deliver 5 shoot-ready ideas to you before your session date.

Can I customize a package to fit my specific needs?

Absolutely. Our packages are starting points. We can adjust deliverables, volume, and scope to fit your exact goals and budget. Reach out via our contact page and we will build something that works for you.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept credit cards, bank transfers, Zelle, and PayPal. A 25% deposit is required across all packages before any pre-production work begins. Corporate retainers are billed monthly after the deposit.

Do you travel outside Philadelphia?

Yes. We are Philadelphia-based but built to travel. Corporate retainer clients and larger projects can include on-location shoots wherever your brand needs us. A small travel fee may apply for shoots beyond 70 miles to cover tolls and gas. We will always let you know upfront before anything is confirmed.

Can I upgrade my package after starting?

Yes, any time. If you want to add episodes, expand your retainer tier, or add services mid-engagement, we will adjust your package and credit any payments already made toward the new total.
