Our Story

Founded in 2023 by Buhzz Taylor and Mike Salvi, XLNZ Productions is Philadelphia's premier video production studio, specializing in podcasts, comedy, films, documentaries, and commercial content.

With a combined experience of over 15 years in the entertainment industry, our founders bring a unique blend of creativity and technical expertise to every project.

Our state-of-the-art facility in the heart of Philadelphia is equipped with the latest technology to bring your vision to life.
