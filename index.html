About XLNZ Productions

We're a full-service video production studio based in Philadelphia, dedicated to bringing your stories to life.

Founded in 2023 by Buhzz Taylor and Mike Salvi, XLNZ Productions has quickly grown to become one of Philadelphia's most comprehensive video production facilities. Our team of creative professionals brings years of experience in every aspect of production, from concept development to final delivery.

Our state-of-the-art facility in the heart of Philadelphia features multiple recording studios, editing suites, and a sound stage designed to accommodate projects of any scale. We specialize in creating compelling content that resonates with audiences and achieves our clients' goals.

Whether you're launching a podcast, producing a commercial, or creating a documentary, our team is committed to delivering exceptional results that exceed your expectations.
