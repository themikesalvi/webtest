Who We Are

The Story Behind XLNZ Productions

Founded in 2023 by Buhzz Taylor and Mike Salvi, XLNZ Productions is Philadelphia's premier video production studio. We're not just creators — we're storytellers who bring creative visions to life.

From our state-of-the-art facility in Philly, we produce podcasts, comedy specials, films, documentaries, and commercial content that captivates audiences and delivers real results.
