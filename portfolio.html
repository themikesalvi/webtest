Featured Project

Our most recent and proud production

Philadelphia Food Scene Documentary

A comprehensive exploration of Philadelphia's diverse culinary landscape, featuring interviews with renowned chefs, visits to iconic eateries, and a deep dive into the city's food culture. This 45-minute documentary showcases the passion and creativity that makes Philadelphia a food destination.

Our team spent three months capturing the essence of the city's food scene, from bustling Reading Terminal Market to hidden gems in neighborhood corners. The project involved extensive research, multiple location shoots, and careful post-production to bring the story to life.

Completed: March 2023
Duration: 45 minutes
Format: 4K Documentary
Awards: Philadelphia Film Festival Official Selection
